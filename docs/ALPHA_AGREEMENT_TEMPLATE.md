## 4. Participant Commitments
The Participant agrees to:
*   Provide access to relevant documentation (model cards, oversight policies).
*   Provide an anonymized dataset for bias testing (min. 1,000 records).
*   Appoint a "Human-in-the-Loop" lead for interviews.
*   **Case Study:** Allow AIC to produce an anonymized case study of the pilot process for investor and regulatory demonstration.

## 5. AIC Commitments
AIC agrees to:
*   Maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary model architecture.
*   Provide a free upgrade to the full **SANAS-Accredited Certification** (valid for 1 year) once accreditation is finalized.
*   Provide direct support for Information Regulator inquiries related to the audited system.

## 6. Confidentiality & Data Privacy
AIC will not store raw PII (Personally Identifiable Information). All data for bias testing must be de-identified by the Participant prior to transfer. Both parties agree to handle all project communications under a standard Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).
