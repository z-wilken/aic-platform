Human-Decisive

Mandatory Human Judgment

AI may assist with information gathering, but final decisions must be made by qualified humans. Reserved for high-stakes, irreversible decisions where consequences are severe and human judgment is irreplaceable.

Certification Requirements

  • 100% human review of critical decisions with documented sign-off
  • Professional credentials and liability insurance for all decision-makers
  • AI restricted to advisory role with no autonomous decision authority
  • Audit trails proving human intervention before decisions take effect
  • Ethics review board with regular case audits
2

Human-Supervised

AI Execution with Oversight

AI can execute decisions, but humans must be able to intervene, override, and explain outcomes. Applied to consequential but reversible decisions where AI efficiency is valuable but human oversight remains essential.

  • Quarterly bias testing across protected classes with statistical significance
  • Continuous monitoring systems with automated drift detection
  • Response time commitments for human review requests
3

Automated-Permissible

AI-Driven with Monitoring

AI can operate autonomously for low-stakes, reversible decisions with periodic oversight. Maximum efficiency for routine operations where consequences are minimal and decisions can be easily reversed.
