# AIC Vision Realignment Log

**Date:** February 17, 2026
**Purpose:** Record of all documentation changes made to realign AIC's identity from "POPIA Section 71 compliance body" to "global benchmark for human accountability in automated decisions — starting in South Africa."

---

## Core Realignment

**Previous framing (now deprecated as primary identity):**
> "AIC is a POPIA Section 71 compliance certification body for South African AI deployments."

**Correct framing (AIC's actual vision):**
> "AIC is the global benchmark for ensuring no final decision about a human being is made solely by a machine. We certify that human empathy, emotion, and accountability remain in the loop — everywhere, for every consequential automated system, not just AI."

**The three-layer architecture that governs all framing:**
```
Layer 1: The 5 Algorithmic Rights (universal, jurisdiction-agnostic)
            ↓
Layer 2: The 3-Tier Accountability Framework (maps rights to decision stakes)
            ↓
Layer 3: Regulatory Mappings (POPIA Sec 71, EU AI Act, Title VII, LGPD, etc.)
```

POPIA Section 71 sits at Layer 3. It is AIC's market entry regulatory anchor in South Africa. It is not the mission. The mission is universal.
